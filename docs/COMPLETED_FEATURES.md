## Summary

The platform covers the main elements of the Service Flow Chart:

- **Assessments**: GAD7, PHQ9, PCL-5, Risk, SDQ
- **Goals & Planning**: Wellbeing plan, weekly goals, progress
- **Support**: Internal and external signposting
- **Therapy**: Bookings, feedback, team profiles
- **Workshops**: Cultural activities, express interest
- **Reflection**: Session and weekly reflection
- **Culturally sensitive & trauma-informed** positioning
- **Engagement**: Thrive Tokens, rewards, voice call, chat
